Problem

Greasy Pizza Boxes Cannot be Recycled

Did you know...

  • 26 million pizza boxes end up in landfills each year.
  • Cardboard in landfills emits dangerous methane gas.
  • Grease from pizza weakens paper fibers and ruins all recyclable paper.
  • Grease and food contaminants on the box promote growth of MAGGOTS!
  • Trees that have been growing for 60 years are cut down to make pizza boxes that are used for only 60 minutes.

Solution

The MI-ECO Pizza Box
  • Easy peel off tin foil on the base of the box
  • Edible glue is used to stick foil to the base (Corn starch with water)
  • Foil has vents to match box vents to let the steam out
  • Box comes ready made from packaging company
  • Box comes with recycling facts about cardboard

Benefits

  • 100% recyclable, reusable, and inexpensive pizza box
  • Saves trees and 26 million boxes from landfill
  • Keeps pizza hot, preserves taste, and lets steam out
  • Box can be recycled 5-7 times
  • Greasy aluminum foil can be recycled
  • Spreads awareness about recyclability of pizza boxes
